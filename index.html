Ellectra IoT Workshop 2026

Work shop is conducted on 20.02.2026

An intensive Internet of Things (IoT) Workshop is to be conducted by Ellectra Company in collaboration with Node Nova, exclusively for the students of the EEE and ECE departments at Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College on Friday, 20.02.2026. The workshop aims to provide hands-on exposure to IoT technologies, embedded systems, and real-time applications. Participants will be introduced to the fundamentals of IoT architecture, sensor integration, microcontroller programming, cloud connectivity, and real-time data monitoring. The session will focus on bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation.
